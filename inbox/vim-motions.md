Q: i
A: This key switches to insert mode and allows you to edit text right before the current cursor position.

---

Q: I
A: This key switches to insert mode and places the cursor at the beginning of the line.

---

Q: a
A: This key switches to insert mode and allows you to edit text right after the current cursor position.

---

Q: A
A: This key switches to insert mode and places the cursor at the end of the line.

---

Q: o
A: This key switches to insert mode adding a line below the current one.

---

Q: O
A: This key switches to insert mode adding a line above the current one.

---

Q: v
A: This key switches to insert mode and places the cursor at the beginning of the line

---

Q: V
A: This key switches to insert mode and places the cursor at the beginning of the line

---

Q: <C-v>
A: This key switches to insert mode and places the cursor at the beginning of the line
